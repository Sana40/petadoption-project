Our adoption process

Thank you for your interest in adopting a pet from us!

Our animal shelter gives you the opportunity for dog adoption, cat adoption, and other animals.

We are dedicated to bringing pets and owners together to create a humane community.

Browse the website or view animals in person at our facility.
Requirements

Adoption Fees and others requirements

Must be 18 years of age or older (or accompanied by an adult) with a current photo ID.

Must be able and willing to spend the time and money necessary to provide training, medical treatment, and proper care for your adopted companion.

Contact us about fees for dogs cats and other animals!

Important to know

Adoptions included

  • FVRCP Vaccination (cats 8 weeks and older)

  • Dewormer

  • One Frontline Treatment

  • Microchip

  • Free 30-day Pet Health Insurance Coverage

  • Transition bag of premium Hill's Science Diet food at adoption

  • DA2PP Vaccination(s) (dogs 8 weeks and older)

  • Heartworm test (dogs 6 months and older)
